Recipes

Vegetable and Tofu Stirfry:
With lightly stirfried broccoli, carrot, mushroom, onion and tofu in a spicy thai sauce, you will love the taste and feel great after eating.

Falafel and Salad wrap:
My personal favourite, taking only a few minutes to prepare and tasting delicious, the fun is in picking your favourite fillings - I suggest iceberg lettuce, fresh tomato, alfalfa sprouts, cucumber, jalepeno peppers and a bit of lemon juice to go with the fried Falafel.

Penne with Nutmeat and homemade pasta sauce:
Great for making in bulk and eating throughout the week or bringing to class or work, the onion, garlic and tomato pasta sauce brings a delicious flavour to the high fibre penne and the high protein nutmeat. Even better seasoned with additional spice to taste.

Easy Nachos:
This is my recipe of choice when I don't feel like cooking. Ready in under five minutes, I can bring the ingredients to work and cook and eat it on a lunchbreak. If you select healthy cornchips it's healthy and nutritious too!
