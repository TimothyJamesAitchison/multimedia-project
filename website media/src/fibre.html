Fibre is an integral part of a balanced diet, however the vast majority of people are deficient in it (97% of Americans according to https://www.drcarney.com/blog/entry/97-of-people-are-deficient-in-this-nutrient ) Fortunately, vegan diets are naturally full of high-fibre foods:

Wholemeal Pasta:
100g of Wholemeal pasta has 8g of Fibre

Falafel:
100g of Falafel has 5.5g of Fibre

Broccoli:
100g of Broccoli has 2.6g of Fibre

Brown Rice:
100g of Brown rice has 3.9g of Fibre
