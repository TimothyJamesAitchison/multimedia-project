Vitamins

There are some vitamins that are important to watch on a vegan diet, whether by focusing on foods containing the vitamin, or by taking supplements.

Vitamin B12

So Good brand "Essential" Soy Milk
So Good makes a soy milk that is fortified with many of the vitamins that are necessary and often lacking in a vegan diet, including 1 microgram of B12 per 250ml serve

Nutmeat:
Nutmeat is enriched with Vitamin B12 (1.7 micrograms per serve)

Iron

If you are female and between the ages of approx 19 and 50, you will require a substantial amount of iron in your diet (see calculator for exact number)

So Good brand "Essential" Soy Milk
So Good makes a soy milk that is fortified with many of the vitamins that are necessary and often lacking in a vegan diet, including 2.3 mg of iron per 250ml serve

Broccoli:
Broccoli has 0.7mg of iron per 100g


If you still struggle to get enough, you may consider taking a supplement.
