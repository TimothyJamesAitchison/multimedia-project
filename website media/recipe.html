Vegetable and Tofu Stirfry:
With lightly stirfried brocoli, carrot, mushroom, onion and tofu in a spicy thai sauce, you will love the taste and feel great after eating.

Ingredients

Broccoli:
1 head of broccoli, roughly chopped

Tofu:
300g of Firm Tofu, we recommend Bean Supreme available from most supermarkets. Ensure the tofu is as firm as possible, as soft tofu will have an undesirable texture
